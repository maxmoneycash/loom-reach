# PRODUCT.md — Loom Reach

## Register
**Product** (design serves the task). It's an instrument, not a landing page — the UI disappears into the decision.

## Purpose
Turn a SKU's sales history into a cross-validated demand forecast with honest uncertainty, then solve the newsvendor production quantity that minimizes the real cost of being wrong (markdowns/deadstock vs. lost margin). A working build of the "Loom Reach — demand sensing" module Anatar announced but hasn't shipped.

## Users & context
- **Primary:** an apparel brand's planner / founder deciding *how many units to cut* for a season. Often on a phone, on a factory floor or between meetings — so mobile is a first-class surface, not an afterthought.
- **Secondary:** a quant/technical reviewer who will scrutinize the forecasting rigor (model competition, CV, MASE/WAPE, Croston for intermittency).
- Job-to-be-done: replace "last season +10%" gut calls with a defensible quantity and its uncertainty.

## Brand personality
Precise · industrial · honest. The tool reads like a **technical drawing / tech-pack** — the language Anatar's own craft (cut-and-sew, patternmaking) already speaks. Confidence through exactness, not decoration. Shows its work (leaderboard, backtest) and admits when naive wins.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboard (card-grid + gradient hero-metric).
- shadcn default look (it's on half the web — reads templated).
- Warm cream/sand "editorial" AI palette.
- Crypto/web3 flavor.

## Accessibility
Body text ≥ 4.5:1. Full keyboard/focus states. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored on every animation. Touch targets ≥ 44px on mobile.
